Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account, along with the expense records you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic usage information, such as login timestamps, to help keep the service secure and reliable.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide the core features of Spendly: logging expenses, generating category breakdowns, and displaying spending summaries. We may also use aggregated, non-identifying information to understand how the app is used and to improve its features over time. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and are only accessible to you through your authenticated account. We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your data is removed in accordance with our standard deletion practices.

Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the application, and these providers only process data as needed to deliver the service.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
